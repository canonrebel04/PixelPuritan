Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering an open and welcoming environment. All participants are expected to be respectful.

Standards
- Be considerate, respectful, and collaborative.
- No harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.

Scope
- Applies to all project spaces, including issues, PRs, and discussions.

Enforcement
- Report incidents to maintainers via the contact in `SECURITY.md`.
- Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal.
